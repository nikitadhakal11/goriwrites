I could hear the shouting in the room above us with the banging and slamming of doors.

Posted on February 19, 2016

I could hear the shouting in the room above us with the banging and slamming of doors. The sounds were so loud as if one was following other vexing and harming them. I was scared, a grown teenager like me was afraid of the noises then what would that little girl be feeling who was sitting in front of me trying to solve maths problems. We knew, We were aware of what was going on,we knew how the argument between her parents was gradually turning into a fight. Maybe she was scared too, that’s why without even having a hungry belly she kept on shouting,... “Mummy,come down” “Mom, I am so hungry” “Mom, we need water, we are thirsty “ “Mom?? Mom??” My heart ached for that little girl who was growing up in such environment. And Maybe I was a little relieved that I wasn’t brought up in such household She knew her fathers angriness also about her mothers madness. Maybe she was worried about both of them,but I was worried about her. The impact causing on her made me vexed at her parents for shouting out loud,for showing the ugliness of broken bonds in front of her,for slamming the door while she was studying in the next room, for ignoring her needy calls ,for not listening her urge to stop the fight. Not all families are perfect. But its the parents duty to display whats only good in front of their children. As even a slightest raise in voice causes a huge impact in their brain. As even a slightest scratch in the bonds fears them as walking in the broken bridge. As even a slightest slamming of door makes them shut their feeling for life. That little fight for you plays as a war in their developing brain making them believe its okay to be shouted by their spouse,its okay to raise the voice ,its okay to disrespect another person. Even the worst of worst becomes okay for them.

Society taught the little me, “Men are supposed to be strong”.

Posted on February 19, 2016

Society taught the little me, “Men are supposed to be strong”. I saw how men are never supposed to cry,how they should never feel any pain,how they should handle every difficulties. And I hated it, I hated how men never washed their pains through their eyes, how they were never seen vulnerable, how their throats never feel trapped whenever they were arguing.... But He wasn’t the men, I heard like. He was delicate as wild roses waving at me from the middle of the field, He told me about the pain he had been enduring, He told me all the secrets, I never thought he would be hiding, He told me about the nights he cried himself to sleep, He cared about the tiniest things,he hold onto the stone where I wrote our names together. He gave me the warmest smile which I never thought a guy could ever give. He looked at me with the happiest eyes, He showed me the calmest ,soft heart while I thought they were supposed to have a stoned heart. He hold my hand during his hard times, He asked for my help when things went downhill, He hugged me tight when he didnt know what to say, He caressed me, And He broke the stereotype of the men this society had imprisoned upon me, But He was as strong and brave as men are Still he was as soft and calm as men are. He is beautiful,the loveliest creature I have seen,and those moles enhances his beauty.

The wounds are supposed to bleed and pus are natural part of healing the wound.

Posted on February 19, 2016

The wounds are supposed to bleed and pus are natural part of healing the wound. They are disgusting and painful still without their discharge no wound can heal.This is what healing is. Burying every emotions deep inside the oceans of my heart,I kept that pain alive unknowingly letting it grow. Shutting myself inside the empty walls of the dark room,eyes bawled out soaking dry, but crying wasn’t the only solution. Yet, the foolish kid inside convinced how silencing every emotions is the only way.... All these years pass by,still when the night gets cold and mornings foggy, picture of your face from different memories we shared appears in front of me,poking my heart that I can never see that face close again. My heart is still heavy with the pain you left me,this grief goes and grows with every people I encounter,hindering me from being the old me. With all the courage left sometimes I pick up my cellphone,dial your number blindfold,wondering what if I press that green button. “Will I hear a hello? Or will you not pick it up? But never had the courage to press the green light until I’m too high to remember. But sometimes I wish I was too high or pretending so atleast I could hear you breathe like I used to sometime. I know I should face the grief of losing you properly so that I can finally let myself go and move on, so I wont be stuck anymore but I don’t want to. I like missing you and being stuck on you. Yah it burns my heart and soul that I could die, Still I want to keep missing you. Although I have plethora of things and this heaviest grief yet to expose to you, But If oneday somehow we were supposed to meet Instead of bleating and vexing I would, Sit by your side and Tell you about my new favorite drama and ask if you have watch it or not, then would recommend you every dramas that made me think "Wish I could tell him about this drama, he would have enjoy this one.Ugh!! Wish I could tell him about this scene.Damn, this is so funny,wish I could tell him,he would have also laugh rightnow." Nothing more.
